My thoughts on the blog





I completely lost a lot of my files and word documents last week when I began to start on the blog. I was really annoyed about this but has taught me a great lesson on time management, and how to correctly use GitHub so this doesn’t happen.

I am quite happy with how the blog has turned out in terms of responsiveness. I do think the interface could have been improved as I think I appears too big and ‘clunky’.

I think I did an okay job on the style system; however my biggest issue was my time management. A key ingredient to making good code is time, which was something I didn’t have and caused me to cut corners in a way that I was not proud of. I hope whoever is reading my blog found it relatively okay to navigate around without too much difficulty and enjoyed their time on my site!

Cheers, Issy
