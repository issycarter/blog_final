Assignment two reflection





Assignment two project was creating a product that could be code-able in a team. I was put in a team of five people, consisting of Christian, Cody, Shiv, Hannah, and Myself. Together we created UXHUB. The purpose of UXHUB is to create an article and content sharing platform that connects people from a wide range of backgrounds who have a passion for User Experience design. Users can read, share, and upload content that is relevant to their needs and is categorised using content filters for their convenience.

Our team unfortunately dealt with a lot of setbacks, which resulted in a slow progression of our work initially. Towards the end we re-gained our momentum and it turned out well.

I considered myself the group leader and keeping the team on track. I was helping out with all of the components in the project. This includes, wireframing, coding, the writeup, and the presentation. I think it tested my creative skills, critical thinking, and leadership skills. Happy with the team’s result. PS: The submit page and nav bar was done by me!
