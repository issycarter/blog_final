Importance of Wireframing





Wireframes make the design process iterative.

This is such a key aspect to user experience design. Throughout this class I have discovered the importance of wireframing before starting a project.

In my first assignment (linked on this article), I really focused on developing the high fidelity wireframing aspect. After finishing assignment one, I realised that block framing is equally as important as it creates the function of the website, before focusing on the appearance and interface.

I have realised there are always different pathways to solving a problem to meet an end result. Having the time to explore the different options is what makes good design, as it opens up the different ways the user can get from A to B.

You want the user journey to be intuitive, pain free, and direct. Creating that solution takes trial and error, which is why I value wireframes!

Also, please enjoy the Nighthawks painting by Edward Hopper, I am a big fan of his work!
